Who I Am

I'm Manjunatha H, an Embedded Systems Developer from Bengaluru, India. I hold a B.E. in Electrical & Electronics Engineering from Jain Institute of Technology, Davanagere.

My passion lives at the intersection of hardware and software — where every byte counts and every bit has a purpose. I love understanding how things work at the lowest level.

I've built real projects in C that touch file I/O, bitwise operations, binary parsing, and memory management. Currently expanding into embedded protocols like UART, I2C, SPI, and CAN.

I don't wait for a classroom to teach me. I pick a concept, break it down, and build something with it.
